About Me

Hi, I'm Brittany — And I Turn Good Books Into Bestsellers

I didn't start out as a book marketing specialist. I started as a reader who fell in love with stories — and then I met incredible authors who had written amazing books… that no one was buying. It broke my heart.

So I got obsessed. I studied every launch strategy, every Amazon algorithm hack, every bestseller campaign I could find. I tested everything. I failed a lot. And eventually, I cracked the code.

Now, I've helped 30+ authors hit major bestseller lists — including debut novelists with zero platform, self-published authors competing with Big 5 publishers, and traditionally published authors whose publishers didn't market their books.

I've generated over $2 million in author revenue, helped sell more than 500,000 books, and built a reputation as one of the most results-driven book marketing specialists in the industry. But more importantly, I've helped authors quit their day jobs, land speaking gigs, sell foreign rights, and build sustainable careers doing what they love.

FAQ

Questions Authors Always Ask Me

Answered honestly — because transparency matters more than sales pitches.

Do I need a big platform or email list to work with you?

+

Absolutely not. Some of my biggest success stories came from debut authors with zero email list, under 100 social media followers, no industry connections, and no previous publishing credits.

What you DO need:

  • ✓ A professionally edited book
  • ✓ A professional cover that matches genre expectations
  • ✓ A willingness to invest in marketing
  • ✓ The discipline to follow a proven system

Platform is helpful, but it's not required. I'll help you build one as we go.

Will you guarantee I hit the bestseller list?

+

No — and you should run from anyone who does. There are too many variables outside our control (algorithm changes, competitor releases, etc.).

What I CAN guarantee:

  • ✓ You'll have the exact same system my 30+ bestselling clients used
  • ✓ I'll work as hard on your book as I do on my own projects
  • ✓ You'll have a clear, proven plan — not guesswork
  • ✓ If something isn't working, I'll pivot immediately

That honesty is why my clients trust me — and why I have a 100% client satisfaction record.

I'm traditionally published. Can you still help?

+

Yes — and I LOVE working with trad authors. Most publishers only actively market your book for 4-6 weeks (if that). After that, you're on your own.

I've worked with authors published by Penguin Random House, HarperCollins, Simon & Schuster, Hachette, and many mid-size presses. My approach with trad authors:

  • → I don't duplicate what your publisher is already doing
  • → I focus on channels and strategies your publisher isn't leveraging
  • → I coordinate WITH your publisher (not against them)
  • → I build evergreen systems that keep selling long after your publisher has moved on

What if my book has already launched?

+

Even better. Seriously. Some of my favorite projects are "book revivals" — taking a book that launched 6 months or 2 years ago and turning it into a bestseller.

Why post-launch campaigns work:

  • ✓ You already have reviews (social proof)
  • ✓ You've learned what didn't work (we fix it)
  • ✓ You have sales data (we optimize based on it)
  • ✓ There's less pressure than a "one shot" launch

I've taken books that sold 300 copies in their first month and turned them into 15,000+ sales in the re-launch month. It's never too late.

What genres do you work with?

+

I've launched bestsellers across a wide range of genres:

Fiction: Thriller / Psychological Thriller, Romance (contemporary, historical, paranormal, romantic suspense), Fantasy (epic, urban, romantasy), Science Fiction, Mystery / Crime, Women's Fiction, Historical Fiction, Literary Fiction, Dystopian, Horror

Nonfiction: Self-Help / Personal Development, Business / Leadership, Memoir, Parenting, Health / Wellness, Psychology, Biography, True Crime, Spirituality

About 40% of my clients are nonfiction authors. The strategies are slightly different, but just as effective.

The one genre I DON'T work with: Children's books (it's a completely different market and I don't have expertise there).

How long does it take to see results?

+

If we're doing a launch campaign: You'll see results on launch day. Rankings, sales, reviews — it all happens in the first 7 days. The goal is to hit your targets during launch week while building momentum for sustained success.

If we're doing a post-launch revival or evergreen system: You'll see initial movement in 2-3 weeks (new reviews, improved rankings), and significant sales increases within 30-45 days.

Long-term: My goal is to build systems that keep selling your book for years. Authors I worked with in 2021 are still seeing 1,000-5,000 sales per month in 2024. That's the power of evergreen marketing systems.

How many clients do you take on?

+

Maximum 8 per quarter. Here's why: I can't give you white-glove, obsessive service if I'm juggling 30 clients.

When I'm working on your campaign:

  • → I'm monitoring your rankings multiple times per day
  • → I'm personally coordinating with newsletter partners, bloggers, podcasters
  • → I'm writing your emails, pitches, and social content (if you're done-for-you)
  • → I'm available via email daily

I can't do that for 30 authors at once. What this means for you: If you're reading this and you want to work together, don't wait. I regularly have a 4-8 week waitlist.

Do you work with authors outside the US?

+

Yes! I've worked with authors in the UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, India, South Africa, Ireland, and many other countries.

The only requirement: Your book must be available on Amazon (any marketplace — .com, .co.uk, .ca, .com.au, etc.).

All communication happens via email. Time zones have never been an issue — I'm flexible and responsive regardless of where you're located.

What if I have multiple books or a series?

+

Even better. Series authors are some of my favorite clients because we can create powerful reader funnels.

If you have a series or multiple books, we can:

  • ✓ Use Book 1 as a funnel (perma-free or $0.99) to hook new readers
  • ✓ Launch your newest book while simultaneously reviving your backlist
  • ✓ Build email funnels that upsell readers through your entire catalog
  • ✓ Create "binge-worthy" launch campaigns that encourage readers to buy all books at once

One of my clients launched Book 5 of her series, and Books 1-4 sold an additional 31,000 copies in the same month. That's the power of strategic series marketing.

How do I know if my book is "ready" to work with you?

+

Your book is ready if:

  • ✓ It's professionally edited (developmental edit, line edit, proofread — no typos, no plot holes)
  • ✓ It has a professional cover designed by someone who understands genre conventions
  • ✓ It's properly formatted for ebook and/or print
  • ✓ You have a clear target audience in mind
  • ✓ You're ready to invest in marketing as a serious business expense

Your book is NOT ready if:

  • ✗ It's still in draft form or hasn't been professionally edited
  • ✗ Your cover looks homemade or doesn't match genre expectations
  • ✗ You're not willing to take feedback on positioning/marketing angles

Not sure? Reach out anyway. I'll be honest about whether you're ready or what you need to do first.
